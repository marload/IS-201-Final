Steps to Make My Sourdough

  1. Feed and wake up starter 2-3 days before baking
  2. Add active starter to water, bread flour, and salt to make a dough
  3. Stretch and fold
    • Let rest 30 minutes
    • Do 2 more folds over an hour and a half
    • Let dough rest for 1.5-3 hours
  4. Shape dough
  5. Cold proof in the fridge overnight (12-18 hours)
  6. Bake in the morning!
